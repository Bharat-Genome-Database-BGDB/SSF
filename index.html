SivaSakthi Science Foundation

At SivaSakthi Science Foundation, our vision is a world where the power of science and education knows no boundaries, where every individual has the opportunity to reach their fullest potential, and where knowledge catalyzes positive global change. Our unwavering mission is to ignite a brighter future through the relentless promotion of science and education. We are driven by the belief that knowledge is the cornerstone of progress, and access to quality education and scientific exploration is the key to unlocking the boundless potential within every individual and society.

Our Latest Project

Our latest endeavor is the Bharat Genome Database , a comprehensive resource created by Indians to catalog and explore India's rich and diverse genomic landscape.

Our Mission

At the Bharat Genome Database, we are on a mission to unlock the genetic secrets of Bharat's most diverse and unique life forms – our indigenous species

Our commitment

We believe in equal access to quality education. Our programs bridge the gap for underprivileged communities, ensuring everyone has the opportunity to learn and thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a comprehensive and accessible repository of genomic information for indigenous species from every corner of Bharat.
