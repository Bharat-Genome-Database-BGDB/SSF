About Sivasakthi Science Foundation

Established with a vision to bridge the gap between science and society, Sivasakthi Science Foundation brings together passionate professionals, educators, and students. Our commitment is to foster curiosity, support research, and create opportunities for learning and collaboration. We believe in the transformative power of science to shape a better future for all.
