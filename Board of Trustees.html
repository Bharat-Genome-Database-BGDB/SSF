Our Leadership Team

The Board of Trustees of our foundation comprises a diverse group of distinguished individuals who bring a wealth of experience and expertise in science, education, and non-profit management. Their collective vision and leadership guide our foundation’s mission to support science research, training, and education.

Dr. Sabarinath Subramaniam

Chairperson

Dr. Subramaniam is a renowned scientist with over 30 years of experience in life sciences. As Chairperson of the Board, Dr. Subramaniam provides strategic oversight and ensures that the foundation remains true to its mission.
