Why Get Involved?

Sivasakthi Science Foundation welcomes individuals and organizations committed to the progress of science. Whether you are a researcher, educator, student, or supporter, your involvement is vital to our mission of promoting scientific research, training, and education.

Volunteer Opportunities

Support our initiatives by volunteering your expertise and time. Volunteers play a key role in the successful execution of our programs.

  • Step 1: Complete the volunteer application form (available soon).
  • Step 2: Attend an orientation session.
  • Step 3: Engage in projects aligned with your skills and interests.

Areas: Event Coordination, Administrative Support, Outreach, Research Assistance.

Partnerships

We collaborate with organizations that share our dedication to scientific advancement. Partnership opportunities include joint research, educational programs, and event sponsorship.

  • Collaborative Research Initiatives
  • Educational Workshops and Programs
  • Event and Conference Sponsorship

For partnership inquiries: info@sivasakthifoundation.org
