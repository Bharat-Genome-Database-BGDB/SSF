Make a Difference with Sivasakthi Science Foundation

Advance science research, training, and education. Join us and be part of the change.

Contact Us

At Sivasakthi Science Foundation, everyone can play a role in advancing science research, training, and education. Whether you are a scientist, educator, student, or simply passionate about making a positive impact, there are many ways to get involved and support our mission.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and contribute your time and skills to support our programs and initiatives. Volunteering with us is a rewarding experience that allows you to make a tangible difference in the scientific community.

  • Step 1: Fill out our volunteer application form (coming soon).
  • Step 2: Attend a volunteer orientation session.
  • Step 3: Start contributing to projects that match your interests and skills.

Volunteer Roles:

  • Event Planning and Support
  • Administrative Assistance
  • Outreach and Education
  • Research Assistance
Partner with Us

We are always looking to build partnerships with organizations that share our commitment to science research, training, and education. By partnering with us, you can help amplify our impact and reach a broader audience.

  • Collaborative Research Projects
  • Educational Programs and Workshops
  • Sponsorship of Events and Conferences

Contact us: info@sivasakthifoundation.org

Attend Our Events

Participate in our events and conferences to learn, network, and contribute to the scientific community. Our events provide a platform for knowledge exchange and collaboration among researchers, educators, and students.

Spread the Word

Help us raise awareness about our mission and work by sharing our content on social media and with your network. Follow us and engage with our posts to increase our reach.

Internships and Fellowships

We offer internship and fellowship opportunities for students and early-career professionals to gain hands-on experience in science research, education, and non-profit management.
