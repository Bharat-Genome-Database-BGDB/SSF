Events and Conferences

At Sivasakthi Science Foundation, we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of scientists, educators, and enthusiasts through our events and conferences. These gatherings provide opportunities for networking, learning, and collaboration, and they play a crucial role in advancing our mission to support science research, training, and education.
