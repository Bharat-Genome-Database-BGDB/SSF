Welcome to Sivasakthi Foundation

Our vision is a world where the transformative power of science and education is accessible to all. We believe knowledge is the cornerstone of progress, and our mission is to catalyze positive change through research, education, and community engagement.

Education Initiatives

Featured Project

Bharat Genome Database

Our flagship initiative, the Bharat Genome Database, is a comprehensive resource cataloging India's rich genomic diversity. Created by Indian scientists, it empowers research and discovery for the nation and beyond.
