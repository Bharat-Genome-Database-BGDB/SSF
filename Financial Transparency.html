Financial Transparency

Our commitment to openness, accountability, and integrity in all financial matters. Building trust with our donors, partners, and communities.

Commitment to Transparency and Accountability

At Sivasakthi Science Foundation, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of financial transparency and accountability. We believe that openness and honesty in our financial practices are essential to building trust with our donors, partners, and the communities we serve.

Our Financial Principles

  • Integrity: We adhere to strict ethical standards in all our financial operations.
  • Accountability: We are accountable to our donors, partners, and beneficiaries for the use of funds.
  • Transparency: We provide clear and accessible information about our financial activities.
  • Community Focus: We ensure resources are used for maximum positive impact.
